Who we are

Fable K9, Lower Basic, Middle Basic, Upper Basic and Secondary

Welcome to Hikimah International Schools, Sokoto.

At Hikimah International Schools, we are committed to providing a nurturing and stimulating environment where children from Kindergarten through Secondary level can thrive academically and personally. Situated behind Abanurat Filling Station in Kwanawa Sokoto, our institution prides itself on being a beacon of knowledge, harmony, and innovation in education.


Our Programs:

Early Childhood (KG 1-2 and Nursery): Our early childhood program embraces the Montessori method, fostering curiosity and foundational skills in a vibrant and engaging setting. Primary Education (Lower Basic, Middle Basic, Upper Basic): We provide a comprehensive curriculum designed to build strong academic foundations, critical thinking, and social skills. Secondary Education: Our secondary school offers a challenging curriculum that prepares students for higher education and the global workforce.

What We Offer

Proficient Administrative Structure: Our experienced administrative team ensures that the school operates smoothly and efficiently. Blended Learning Skills: We integrate traditional and digital learning techniques to create a dynamic educational experience. State-of-the-Art Facilities:
  • ICT Lab: Equipped with the latest technology to enhance digital literacy.
  • Science Lab: Fully equipped for practical, hands-on scientific exploration.
 Expert Guidance and Counseling: Our professional counselors support students in their academic and personal development. Qualified and Disciplined Teachers: Our educators are well-trained to provide quality education and uphold our high standards. Security and Safety: 24/7 CCTV cameras ensure a secure environment within the school premises. Conducive Learning Environment: Our classrooms and outdoor areas are designed to be neat, welcoming, and conducive to learning. Transport Services: Reliable school bus services for safe and convenient pickup and drop-off.

At Hikimah International Schools, we believe in7 holistic education that nurtures each child's unique abilities and potential. Join us in our mission to create lifelong learners and leaders of tomorrow.

We invite you to visit our campus and discover why Hikimah International Schools is the perfect place for your child's education. Forms for admission are available at our school premises. Get your form now and become a part of our vibrant educational community!
