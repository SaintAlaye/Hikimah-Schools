Our Classes

Our school program has five dedicated classes

KG and Nursery Classes

KG and Nursery classes provide a nurturing environment focused on play-based learning, introducing foundational concepts through activities that promote social, emotional, and cognitive development, fostering a love for learning early on.

CLASS SIZE: 20

Lower Basic Classes

Lower Basic classes build a strong academic foundation in literacy, numeracy, & basic sciences through interactive lessons, fostering creativity, collaboration, & critical thinking to prepare pupils for the next phase of their education.

CLASS SIZE: 30

Middle Basic Classes

Middle Basic classes emphasize critical thinking & problem-solving as students explore more complex subjects, deepening their understanding & preparing them for Upper Basic classes through active participation & independent learning.

CLASS SIZE: 30

Upper Basic Classes

Upper Basic classes offer a rigorous academic experience with advanced topics and interdisciplinary learning, fostering inquiry and innovation to prepare students for secondary education and personal development.

CLASS SIZE: 25

Secondary Classes

Secondary classes provide a comprehensive education with a diverse curriculum, preparing students for higher education & careers by developing critical thinking, leadership skills through core subjects & extracurricular activities.

CLASS SIZE: 25
