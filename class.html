    CLASSES

KG and Nursery Classes

Class Size: 20

KG and Nursery classes provide a nurturing environment focused on play-based learning, introducing foundational concepts through activities that promote social, emotional, and cognitive development, fostering a love for learning early on.

Lower Basic Classes

Class Size: 25

Lower Basic classes build a strong academic foundation in literacy, numeracy, and basic sciences through interactive lessons, fostering creativity, collaboration, and critical thinking to prepare pupils for the next phase of their education.

Middle Basic Classes

Class Size: 30

Middle Basic classes emphasize critical thinking and problem-solving as students explore more complex subjects, deepening their understanding and preparing them for Upper Basic classes through active participation and independent learning.

Upper Basic Classes

Class Size: 25

Upper Basic classes offer a rigorous academic experience with advanced topics and interdisciplinary learning, fostering inquiry and innovation to prepare students for secondary education and personal development.

Secondary Classes

Class Size: 25

Secondary classes provide a comprehensive education with a diverse curriculum, preparing students for higher education and careers by developing critical thinking, leadership skills through core subjects and extracurricular activities.
